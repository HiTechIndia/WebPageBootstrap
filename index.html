                    It will also be proper to mention that we have for the first time introduced the Water Heating
                    System for TMT Bars. This is totally an indigenous design where the water is boiled upto 101°C
                    in a Stainless Steel container.
                    The TMT samples are placed in the Stainless Steel wire mesh and boiled for 30 minutes a Timer Circuit and
                    Pyrometer is provided in the control panel of the system. The samples are cooled in normal ambient temperature and
                    tested for Rebend.

                    Furthermore IS : 1786 – 2012 specifies 0.2% Proof Stress / Yield Stress for all Grades of TMT / Tor /
                    CTD Bars / Flats & Angles.
                    With the Electronic Extensometer the results are directly displayed online , on the PC and 0.1% or
                    0.2% Proof Stress can be evaluated and plotted on a Printer.
